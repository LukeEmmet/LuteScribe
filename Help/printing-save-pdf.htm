Preview, printing and exporting a PDF

From LuteScribe you can preview your output, and when required print or export a PDF of the current piece.

To preview your file, choose File->Preview PDF or simply switch to the Print view tab. This will show a preview of your current piece - you can zoom in and out or page through the preview output.

The File->Export->Export as PDF command creates a PDF in the same folder as your tabulature file. 

To update the preview following edits, simply switch to this Print view tab, or re-run the Preview PDF menu option.
